What is the cost of preferred stock?
The cost of preffered stock is the rate of return that is required by preffered shareholders. Simply the cost of preferred stock is the price that the company pays in return for the income it gets from selling it's stock.
How to calculate cost of preferred stock?
To calculate cost of preferred stock you should use the following formula:
cost of preferred stock = (dividend / share price) * 100
Example: Company x's preferred stock has an annual dividend of $2.3 and the share price is $19, calcualte the cost of preferred stock.
Using the cost of preferred stock formula the answer will be:
cost of preferred stock = (dividend / share price) * 100
cost of preferred stock = ($2.3 / $19) * 100
cost of preferred stock = 12.1%
Why use cost of preferred stock?
Companies use the cost of preferred stock to know the price it should pay in return for the income that it gets from selling it's stock.
